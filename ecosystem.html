Payment
Open CryptoPay is a licence-free, open P2P payment standard that works across many blockchains and crypto assets, with full backward compatibility to Bitcoin Lightning LNURL. It enables businesses to effortlessly accept digital assets, without the need for blockchain software or specialised hardware. Payments can be processed using existing contactless (NFC) terminals or various wallets. With ultra-low transaction fees - far cheaper than EC, VISA or Mastercard - Open CryptoPay offers a cost-effective, completely permissionless solution. Customers can pay without a debit or credit card, making crypto transactions more accessible and seamless than ever before.
opencryptopay.io
